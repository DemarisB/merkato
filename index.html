Welcome to Merkato Market located at 2157 Queen City Avenue in Cincinnati, Ohio!

We are a grocery store serving the Ethiopian and Eritrean community in the Greater Cincinnati area. We have been a source of Abesha culture and food (including injera, fresh lamb, beef and spices) for the Greater Cincinnati area since 2012.

Discover Merkato!

Merkato Market has a wide selection of fresh injera and himbasha daily!

We carry a fresh selection of lamb, beef and organic chicken as well as traditional spices used to make Abesha foods.
